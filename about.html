The Aurelia Legacy

Redefining Bespoke Brokerage

Aurelia was established with a singular focus: to elevate real estate transaction consulting into a highly personalized art form. We serve developers, collectors, and corporate entities who demand architectural brilliance and absolute transactional privacy.

From our base in London, we have grown systematically to incorporate critical markets like Dubai, New York, and Aspen. Our directors coordinate transactions on off-market deals, securing some of the world's most desired postcodes for our global clientele.

Chronology of Growth

Our Historic Timeline

2016

Foundation In London

Aurelia is founded in Mayfair, London, setting out to serve high-net-worth families with boutique asset consulting, property acquisitions, and private listing representation.

2019

Global Office Expansions

Secured transaction licenses for Dubai and New York, building local teams of specialists to cater to cross-border real estate investments and offshore portfolio managers.

2022

Bespoke Asset Management

Launched our dedicated luxury asset management division, providing end-to-end estate upkeep, staff management, and luxury concierge services for seasonal residences.

2025

$2 Billion Transaction Volume

Surpassed a collective lifetime closed volume of $2,000,000,000, solidifying Aurelia's standing as a premier luxury real estate agency.
