Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers regarding private representation, listing checks, and buying workflows.

How do I access off-market properties?

Over 40% of our luxury properties are represented off-market to protect seller privacy. Registered clients receive personalized digital lookbooks and invitations to private viewing sessions from their assigned acquisitions director.

What legal checks are completed for buying transactions?

Our compliance team completes rigorous land registry title audits, municipal zoning checks, outstanding mortgage analysis, and debt reports. This ensures complete safety and clear title handovers for buyers.

Do you coordinate private travel for site tours?

Yes. For clients viewing high-value assets, we arrange airport concierge arrivals, private helicopter transfers, marine yacht charters, and hotel reservations to ensure a comfortable and confidential trip.

What services are included in property management?

Our property management team handles routine system checks, landscaping, pool cleaning, bill payments, tax updates, security monitoring, and seasonal preparation services for international owners.
