Perched on a premium bluff overlooking the majestic Pacific Ocean, Villa Serene stands as a definitive architectural masterpiece. Designed by renowned luxury home creators, this estate blends raw modern geometry with fluid transitions between indoor comfort and sweeping outdoor ocean scenery.

A private elevator accesses the upper suites, including a primary bedroom retreat equipped with custom oak cabinetry, a private wellness bath, and a private sunset deck.
